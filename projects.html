Projects

  • Fruitpocalypse

    It started as a basic tower defense game I was building for practice. Eventually, I had the idea of combining tower defense mechanics with deck-builders, a genre I was (and still am) very interested in. One of the biggest challenges I faced was implementing complex coroutines on handling the individual cards and dealing the initial hand. While I eventually overcame this obstacle, this game is still in progress, and I hope to be able to make a full release in the future.

    Skills Acquired
    • Pathfinding algorithms
    • Changing editor functionality via scripts
    • Complex coroutines to animate movement of cards
    • Voxel art (MagicaVoxel)
    • Blender

  • RPG Level

    This game is a basic RPG level in which a player must fight their way through a number of guards to reach the other end of a village. The player can pick up health and several different types of weapons to help them along the way. Defeating enemies will allow players to level up, with each weapon responding differently to the increased stats. The guards will patrol, chase, and eventually return to patrolling after losing the player.

    Skills Acquired
    • Interfaces
    • C#/Unity Events
    • Save System
    • Scene transition
    • Levelling system
    • Scriptable objects

  • Zombie Runner

    In this game the player explores a dark landscape infested by zombies, using the weapons and ammo they find along the way to survive. The zombies will react to nearby players or stop when a player is out of range, and their swipes are synchronized with player damage. The player also needs to keep an eye out for batteries or their flashlight might run out!
